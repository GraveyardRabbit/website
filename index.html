Welcome

Hello! My name is Charlie, I have a degree in 3D & VFX: Stylized & Character Design at Digital Arts & Entertainment Howest, and I'm currently majoring in Game Sound Integration.

Throughout my studies, I've built a solid foundation in crafting 3D models and sound effects. Working with CreatorGalaxy, entering game jams, and working on short film projects has helped me improve my skills in asset creation, sound design, and problem-solving in creative workflows. I really enjoy the challenge of bringing visuals and audio together to create something immersive and engaging.
